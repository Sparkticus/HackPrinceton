Banana Bread

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

  • 2. Mix butter into mashed bananas in a large mixing bowl.

  • 3. Mix in the sugar, egg, and vanilla.

  • 4. Sprinkle the baking soda and salt over the mixture and mix in.

  • 5. Add the flour, mix.

  • 6. Pour mixture into a buttered 4x8 inch loaf pan.

  • 7. Bake for 1 hour.

  • 8. Cool on a rack. Remove from pan and slice to serve.
